The Abode of Snow

Discover the Mighty Himalayas

The Himalayas, or "abode of snow," are the highest and most formidable mountain range on Earth. Stretching across five countries, this vast chain is home to over one hundred peaks exceeding 7,200 meters, including the world's highest, Mount Everest. More than just mountains, the Himalayas are a source of life, feeding great rivers and shaping the climate of Asia. For adventurers, they represent the ultimate challenge and a place of profound spiritual significance.
